Action Plan by Severity

1,178 Trees — Mild Stress
  • Apply NPK 12-12-17 fertilizer (500g/tree)
  • Ensure adequate irrigation — 200L/tree/week
  • Inspect for early signs of Rhinoceros Beetle
  • Re-survey in 6 weeks
683 Trees — Severe Stress
  • Trunk injection with Imidacloprid for pest control
  • Apply Magnesium Sulphate (200g) for nutrient boost
  • Check roots for Coconut Root Wilt disease
  • Field visit by agronomist within 2 weeks
137 Trees — Critical / Dead
  • Remove dead trees immediately to prevent disease spread
  • Soil test the affected area before replanting
  • Replant with disease-resistant CRISL Dwarf variety
  • Report to local Coconut Development Authority
☀️

Best time to apply treatments: Early morning (6–9 AM) or late afternoon (4–6 PM). Avoid applying fertilizers during heavy rain. Always wear gloves and follow product label instructions.
